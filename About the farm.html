AgriNexus was built around five real problems facing Rwandan agriculture

Marketplace

Price-transparent market

Small-scale farmers lose income because

they don't know the current market rate

for their crops. This service publishes live,

government-verified commodity prices by

region and connects farmers directly to

consumers — cutting out exploitative

intermediaries.

  • Live regional commodity price tracker
  • Direct farmer-to-consumer listings
  • Government-sourced price feed for accuracy

Field intelligence

Satellite field heat maps

Blanket-spraying an entire field wastes

expensive fertiliser and energy. The

platform integrates satellite imagery to

show a farmer exactly which square

metres of their land need nitrogen, water,

or other nutrients — so resources go only

where needed.

  • Per-square-metre crop health analysis
  • Nutrient and water deficiency overlays
  • Reduces fertiliser and energy costs

AI Advisory

AI-powered crop & climate advisor

Climate change has made traditional planting calendars unreliable. In 2026, farmers face

unpredictable droughts, new pest infestations, and crop diseases they've never encountered

before. This service gives every farmer an AI specialist in their pocket — describe your crop's

condition and get actionable guidance, plus real-time weather alerts so you're never caught off-

guard by sudden changes.

  • Agriculture-specialised AI advisory
  • Pest and disease diagnosis from description
  • Unpredictable drought early warnings
  • Real-time hyper-local weather alerts
  • Planting calendar recommendations
  • Integrates with field heat map data
